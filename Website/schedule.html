Schedule

September, 2023

For september we are hoping to finalize which puzzles we are hoping to use in the room and actually build/connect/integrate them. This includes getting the clock to tick properly, get the latches for the boxes working as they should, and connect all of the components using a Wi-Fi network system. The components should also report back to the main system that they have been completed. We are also hoping to start work on the experiental aspect of the escape room.

October, 2023

Since Halloween is in October we are going to be focusing on the horror aspect of the escape room! Just kidding, it's not because Halloween is in October, but it does line up nicely. During October we are going to focus heavily on how the room is going to play out and what we are hoping the player will feel while they are in the room. That will include lighting, spooky sounds, and further development of the story. We are also going to focus on getting more puzzles developed for the room and we are going to integrate them into the connected system that we developed in September.

November, 2023

By November we are hoping to have a lot of the room completed. We are still going to be adding things and pushing to reach our stretch goals, however, November is going to be utilized to test the room, flush out bugs, and prepare anything else that we may need in order to be fully ready for Demo day.

December, 2023

December, although not very long in terms of the semester, is going to be vitally important. This is the month that we are actually planning on showcasing our room and allowing people to play it on demo day! There may still be a few little bugs or stretch goals that we are working on during December, but it will mostly be used to meditate and reflect on how Demo day is going to go. And to play the room!
